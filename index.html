About the Comic

This richly illustrated comic brings to life the incredible story of Emirates’ rise — narrated by Veronica, who guides readers through four decades of milestones. From the airline’s humble beginnings at Dubai International Airport to bold bets on the A380, record-breaking aircraft orders, global partnerships, and futuristic visions at Al Maktoum International Airport, every chapter captures the spirit of ambition and innovation.
